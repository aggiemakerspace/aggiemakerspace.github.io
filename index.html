Princeton

Since we are at a place where people are not introduced to innovative thinking, we must find a way display the innovative side of life. To present and build "out of the box" concepts that spark the dormant creativity that paves the road for futuristic technology and ultimately ourselves. The focus is building confidence in one’s abilities to the point where they have the creativity, strength and intellect to confidently take on the challenges of life.

Mission Statement

In accordance with the mission statement of North Carolina A&T State University, the mission of the Makerspace is to establish a unique, collaborative environment “dedicated to learning, discovery, and community engagement.” By providing our students with a plethora of equipment, technologies, and resources, we ensure the facilitation of creativity, problem solving, and innovation.
